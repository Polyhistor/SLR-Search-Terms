\subsection{Search strategy}

Table \ref{table:searchTerms} depicts the resulting mapping of databases and search terms. If a term in the “Search Term” column is printed in bold, this indicates a field of the search mask, where the following text is input. In case nothing is printed in bold, this means the whole term can be filled into the search bar without modification. As can be seen, due to the focus of this study, the term “microservice” or its plural always had to be included in the title.
